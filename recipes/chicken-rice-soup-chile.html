Chicken and Rice Soup with Garlicky Chile Oil

Cozy creamy chicken, ideal for winter.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 6 garlic gloves (chopped)
  • 2/3 cup white rice
  • 1/4 cup + 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 2 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 large bunch kale (chopped)
  • 1 lemon

Recipe Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large saucepan over medium
  2. Add garlic and cook for ~3-5 minutes until golden brown while swirling pan
  3. Transfer to a small bowl while leaving 1tbsp garlic oil in pot
  4. Stir crushed red pepper flakes into garlic oil in bowl
  5. Add onion to garlic oil remaining saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring ocassinaly for ~5 minutes till onion is translucent.
  6. Pour 8 cups of water and stir to combine along with chicken and salt
  7. Bring to boil, then reduce heat to low and bring to gentle simmer for ~20 minutes until chicken is cooked thoroughly.
  8. Transfer chicken to plate and shred once cool enough.
  9. Add chicken and kale to pot, while simmering and stirring occasionaly for ~4 minutes till kale is bright green and chicken is warmed.
  10. Remove pot from heat, squeeze in lemon juice and add salt to taste. Stir to combine.
  11. When ready to eat, drizzle in reserved garlic-chile oil to taste and enjoy!
