Miso Soup 味噌汁

Quick and simple miso soup.

Ingredients

  • Dashi
  • Miso Paste
  • Tofu
  • Veggies

Recipe Instructions

  1. Simmer dashi over medium heat
  2. Add 4-5 Tbsp of miso paste to the dashi and stir to dissolve
  3. Chop tofu/veggies and add to miso once completely dissolved
  4. Never boil miou soup as it will lose nutrients, flavor, and aroma.
